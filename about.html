About Me

Introduction - Hello! I am Kelvin, a human-centred computing major! You can call me Kel or KOD (initial names). I graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a Human-Centred Computing (HCC) Bachelor of Science Degree. My concentrations are front-end development and accessibility.
Seeking a full-time job opportunity to work as a UI/UX designer, Usability Tester, and/or UI developer. Seeking to develop systems that are accessible through desktop, web, and mobile computing interfaces for users which benefit.

About Myself - I am deaf and I sign American and British Sign Language. I was born hearing and had hearing loss at three years old. I wore hearing aids since I was three years old, then I got my right ear cochlea implanted at twenty-three years old. I first learned basic signs in pre-kindergarten. It was Pidgin Sign English (PSE), then I learned American Sign Language in high school.

Hobbies - I like cooking, doing hot yoga and circus (contortion, acrobat, trapeze, etc.), playing some games on my personal computer and video game, walking, climbing, hiking, and going out with friends. I am always up for something new.
